About Campus Social Network

Campus Social Network is an online platform designed exclusively for students to connect, share, and collaborate within their college environment. The aim is to create a digital community where students can interact, post updates, share ideas, and stay informed about events.

Whether you're a first-year student or about to graduate, this network helps you stay connected with peers, faculty, and campus happenings in a friendly and secure space.

Connect with Classmates

Find and follow students from your course, batch, or hostel.

Post & Comment

Share your thoughts, project ideas, and achievements with others.

Stay Updated

Get notified about college events, exam dates, and announcements.
